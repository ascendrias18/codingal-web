ABOUT donation.com

donation.com is a website for collection of donation online,connected with many NG0s ,donation.com provides funds which people donate here to them. Managed by many NGOs ,this is a joint website for collection of funds to help society.Here the managing NGOs post their projects and the required amount of fund which then visitors altogether donate. In this website,not only cash but clothes,books,food and many things are donated.Not a penny goes to the site ,donations are collected by NGOs and then distributed to help the poor,to help the society. WITH OUR SLOGAN"DONATE TO HELP THE NEEDY",we are trying to help the needy.
